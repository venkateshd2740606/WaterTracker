## Full Description
WaterTracker is the ultimate screw puzzle experience. Remove screws in the right order to drop layered plates and clear each level.

**Features:**
- Screw Puzzle with procedural level generation (thousands of unique puzzles)
- 5 handcrafted tutorial levels
- 6 difficulties plus Endless mode
- Daily, Weekly, and Monthly challenges with streaks and rewards
- 7 beautiful themes (Light, Dark, AMOLED, Neon, Cyber, Space, Nature)
- XP, levels, ranks, achievements, and coin economy
- Multiplayer: Same device pass-and-play
- Puzzle Profile: On-device play-style analysis and adaptive difficulty
- Seed Lab: Share exact puzzle seeds with friends
- Full accessibility: reduced motion, high contrast, colorblind modes, font scaling
- GDPR/CCPA consent management
